Employer description

Greene King is one of the UK’s leading pub retailers and brewers, with a proud heritage dating back over 200 years.

Operating thousands of pubs, restaurants, and hotels across the country, Greene King hires nationally and offers a wide variety of job opportunities. Roles range from front-of-house positions such as bar staff and servers, to kitchen roles like chefs and kitchen assistants, as well as management and support centre positions.

Greene King is committed to developing its people, offering structured training and career progression. If you're interested in working with Greene King, please speak to your local employment broker who can help you get in touch and guide you through the application process.
